Mishal Zeera is a multi-instrumentalist composer, producer and engineer with a wide range of musical interests. His discography ranges from the demos he did for Jack Savoretti in 2002 to punk and indie bands in London and New York City. Since moving to Haarlem in the Netherlands, his wide interest has had him working the music director of the Haarlem Protestant Church and young punk bands in rehearsal rooms. Local artists he has worked with include Floris Bates and Treasures of Grundo. Mishal is the bass player and producer of The Irrational Library, as well as a singer songwriter.
