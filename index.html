Mishal Zeera is a multi-instrumentalist composer, producer and engineer with a wide range of musical interests. His discography ranges from the demos he did for Jack Savoretti in 2002 to indie bands in London (Kalki, Straylings) and New York City (Deadbeat Darling, Josh Sampson). Since moving to Haarlem in the Netherlands, his interests have had him working with the music director of the Haarlem Protestant Church as well as young punk bands in rehearsal rooms. Mishal is a guitarist, pianist, bassist as well as a singer songwriter.
