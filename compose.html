My philosophy on music is that it should provoke emotions, and it shouldn't be ashamed of itself. It seems to me that the emotional impact of music sounds more and more forced and calculated these days. There seems to be a need for safety in musical exploration. Our human experience, in contrast, is reaching new heights of change and turbulence. As a person, that makes me try to bridge that gap.
