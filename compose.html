Music should provoke emotions, and it shouldn't be ashamed of itself. The emotional impact of music sounds more forced and calculated than ever these days. I personally see no need for safety in musical exploration. Our human experience, in contrast, is reaching new heights of change and turbulence. As a person, that makes me try to bridge that gap.
