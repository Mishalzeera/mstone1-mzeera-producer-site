I believe in well-arranged songs played by musicians who are enjoying themselves. Maybe they are challenging themselves, and you can hear the heroism in that. A good record does so much more than you hear on a first listen, it has a life of its own. If I would try and define my approach, I think its to try and keep that freshness and mystery alive - at every stage of the technical process.
